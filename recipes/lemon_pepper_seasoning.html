Lemon pepper seasoning

Description

This is a seasoning that has many different uses, however it really shines as a dry rub for protiens.

Use this simple three ingredient spice power to pack a punch to your chicken or fish dishes.

I especially recommend this over chicken wings.

    Ingredients:
  • Lemon zest
  • Black peppercorns
  • Flakey salt
    Instructions
  1. Zest your lemon.
  2. Bake you lemon zest at 160C for 25 minutes.
  3. Remove your zest from the oven to cool
  4. Add to a pestle and morter, along with your black pepper corns
  5. Grind this mixture into a semi corse powder
  6. Add your flakey salt
  7. Grind mixture to a fine powder
  8. Toss over fried chicken wings, or rub into protein before cooking
