Tomato sauce

Description

This tomato sauce is a staple in my kitchen, covering everything from pasta to pizza

The quality of the ingredients really come out in this ultra forgiving recipe, give it a try and see!

    Ingredients
  • Plum tomatos
  • Garlic
  • White onions
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Mixed herbs
  1. Cut your tomatos into quarters
  2. Finely slice your white onions
  3. Chop garlic
  4. Add your chopped produce to a roasting tin
  5. Drizzle olive oil over the produce
  6. Add your slat, pepper, and mixed herbs
  7. With your hands, mix ingredients until everything is thoroughly coated
  8. Place in oven at 180C for 35 minutes, or until charred and fragrant
  9. Blend down roasted ingredients until smooth
  10. Serve on top of your favoruite pizza dough or pasta
