Coleslaw

Description

Coleslaw is a popular salad dish, served alongside barbecues and in many burger restaurants.

It is usually comprised of white and red cabbage, grated carrot, and white onion.

    Ingredients
  • White cabbage
  • Red cabbage
  • White onion
  • Grated carrot
  • Mayonaise
    Instructions
  1. Finely slice your red cabbage.
  2. Finely slice your white cabbage.
  3. Finely slice your white onion.
  4. Grate your carrots to your prefered thickness.
  5. Add all of your prepared vegetables to a container.
  6. Add the mayonaise to the container.
  7. Mix until a throughly coated.
  8. Serve alongside your desired dish, Enjoy!
